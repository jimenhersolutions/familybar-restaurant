FAMILY BAR & RESTAURANT

A Taste of Addis Ababa and Asmara in Guelph.

Our Story

Welcome to Family Bar & Restaurant! Here, food is not just a necessity, it's a **communal celebration**. We invite you to dive into the authentic and vibrant flavors of Eritrean and Ethiopian cuisine. From the iconic, spongy Injera, which serves as the base for our rich and aromatic stews (Wat), to the unique and intense spices. Every dish is prepared with love and passion, promising a fun and memorable experience that will delight your senses and make you feel right at home. Get ready for the best food you'll ever share!

FOOD IS A

Every dish tells a story of our Ethiopian and Eritrean heritage, served with vibrant spices and family warmth.

House Specials

Doro Wot

The most famous chicken stew, slow-cooked in a berbere (spice blend) sauce with a hard-boiled egg. Rich and spicy.

Beef Tibsi

Strips of marinated beef sautéed over high heat with onions, peppers, and the unmistakable flavor of Awaze.

Chacha

Tender lamb pieces with some bone, seared on a hot skillet with onions, peppers and garlic. A savory house specialty.

Zighni

Beef stew, infused with berbere and herbs. A deeply flavored, aromatic, and spicy traditional dish.
